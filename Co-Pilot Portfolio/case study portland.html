Portland Airbnb Dashboard Case Study

Having a close connection to Portland, I was particularly interested in exploring how Airbnb listings are distributed across the city and how they contribute to the local community. This project allowed me to combine my passion for data analysis with a real-world application, providing valuable insights into a sector that is both economically significant and rapidly evolving.

This project aims to create a comprehensive dashboard that visualizes Airbnb data for Portland for the years 2024-2025. The dashboard includes various charts to analyze room type listings, revenue trends, and pricing across neighborhoods.

Case Studies

Portland AirBnB Analysis

Role: Analyst & Dashboard Designer

Process: Gathered and cleaned AirBnB data, identified key business questions, and visualized insights in Tableau.

Challenges: Incomplete data, seasonal fluctuations.

Results: Provided actionable recommendations for host pricing and market targeting.

Business Questions

  • What is the distribution of different room types in Portland?
  • Which room types are most popular in different neighborhoods?
  • What are the monthly revenue trends for Airbnb in Portland for 2024-2025?
  • What are the average prices of Airbnb listings in different neighborhoods?

Dashboard Process

Step 1: Data Preparation

Obtained Airbnb data for Portland, Oregon, for the years 2024-2025 directly from the Airbnb website. Ensured the dataset included information on room types, prices, revenue, neighborhoods, and dates. Used tools such as Excel and MySQL to clean the data, remove duplicates and missing values, and standardize date formats.

Step 2: Import Data into Tableau

Connected the cleaned dataset as a CSV/Excel file to Tableau by navigating to the “Data” menu and selecting “New Data Source.”

Step 3: Create the Dashboard

Created individual charts based on the business questions. When all charts were ready, added them to the dashboard. Charts include:

  • Distinct Count of Room Type Listings
  • Revenue by Month
  • Room Type Price by Neighborhood
  • Price by Neighborhood

Step 4: Enhance the Dashboard

  • Interactivity
  • Aesthetics
  • Validation

Step 5: Provide Key Insights

  • Dominance of Entire Home/Apt Listings: Indicates a strong preference for privacy and space. Marketing efforts could emphasize the benefits of having an entire place to oneself, especially for families or larger groups.
  • Seasonal Revenue Trends: Peak months likely correspond to summer. Understanding these trends can help in planning marketing campaigns and adjusting pricing strategies.
  • Neighborhood-Specific Strategies: High-priced neighborhoods might benefit from marketing that emphasizes luxury and exclusive experiences. Lower-priced neighborhoods might highlight value and affordability.
  • Price Variation Across Neighborhoods: Significant price variations suggest a diverse market. High-priced neighborhoods like King and Richmond may be more desirable due to their location or amenities.

Conclusion

Key Takeaways

  • Strategic Pricing and Marketing: Hosts can use the insights from this analysis to adjust their pricing and marketing strategies according to seasonal trends and neighborhood demand to optimize their revenue.
  • Investment Opportunities: Potential investors can identify high-performing neighborhoods and room types to make informed decisions about where to invest in new properties.
  • Enhanced Guest Experience: Understanding the preferences of travelers can help hosts tailor their offerings to better meet guest expectations.

Future Considerations

  • Impact of Regulatory Changes: Future analyses should consider potential regulatory changes affecting short-term rentals in Portland.
  • Longitudinal Data Analysis: Continuing to monitor and analyze data over time will provide deeper insights into long-term trends.
  • Competitive Landscape: Examining the impact of other short-term rental platforms and hotels could offer a more holistic view.
