About Me

Hey, I’m Emmanuel. Born and raised in Oregon, I’ve always had a drive to experience more—whether that means tackling new projects, traveling somewhere unfamiliar, or diving into spicy food (I’ll try anything twice). My curiosity led me to teach myself HTML and eventually combine a love of data, public service, and problem-solving into a career.

For over five years, I’ve worked in business intelligence and emergency management, helping organizations make sense of complex information and turn insight into action. My experience includes leading statewide evaluations, automating reporting systems, and building feedback loops that actually help people do their jobs better and faster. I’m most energized when work brings together data, people, and the chance to do some good.

I’m a firm believer in continuous learning—whether it’s earning new FEMA or analytics certifications, exploring the latest in Power BI and Tableau, or just trying to finish IMDb’s Top 250 movies. When I’m not working, you’ll probably find me at the gym, planning my next trip, listening to podcasts, or seeking out my next favorite local dish.

I thrive on collaboration, clear communication, and a touch of humor. My approach is simple: stay curious, be adaptable, and make a meaningful impact—no matter the challenge.
